<h1> <i> CHOCOLATE </i> </h1>

Chocolate is a sweet food which everyone loves to eat. It is basically made from cocoa beans which is a plant source. Using chocolate we can mke different dishes such as: Chocolate milk, chocolate bars, chocochip cookies etc.

But there is always a disadvantage we should know before eating chocolates. Eating too much of chocolate may lead to cavities,which will make our teeth fall out. Cavities create a lot of pain in the teeth as well as the gums.

So, we should always be alert in the intake of chocolate.

We talked about disadvantages now let's discuss about the advantages of chocolate. It is a really sweet treat thet we can eat when we are bored. Intaking a small amount of chocolate gives us energy as it is a good source of carbohydrates. It also helps us to get back our lack of taste.
